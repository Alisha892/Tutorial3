Impact Of Technology In Watch Industry

Talking about the technology, Technology is the part of our day to day life which refers to the application of the scientific for the practical purposes especially in the field of industries.Technology is the machinery equipment developed from the application of scientific knowledge.Since, techonlogy plays the major role in our daily life likewise, technology brought of a huge change in the watch industry. From the pocket watch to the smart watch technology played the major role.

Positive Imapct Of Technology In Watch Industry

Nowadays, watches has become the way of fashion. For the fashion thing the technology has played a major role in it. Technology has elevated the looks of the watch and made it into a perfection. Some postive impact of the technology in watch industry are as below:

  • Mass Production

    • There used to a time when all watches were handcrafted by skilled craftsmen on large scale but nowadays watches are produced in the factories in a large scale. Since, manufacturing became mechanized the production of watches became cheaper as well.The production of watches became more easy and realiable.

  • Developed Automation

    • With the start of watch making which became mechanized, watchmakers started automating the process. Watches began as simple time pieces, but later, designers started adding new features. From the start of normal wrist watch to the automated watches technology has played a major role in watch industry for the process of the automation.

  • Increased Complexity

    • Technology has brought up the huge change in watches. Now, the watches are not only limited within to see the time but instead it can do everything from showing the time to surfing the internet. Developement of complex smartwatches with the help of technology has made it possible to measure a heart rate, keep a track of your fitness level to sleep schedule.As watches become more intricate, watch makers needed to find ways to make these comples details functional.

  • Enhanced Precision

    • Speaking of precision, when it comes to how watches are made today, precision is everything. Because of this, watch manufacturers are always looking for ways to increase the accuracy of their watches. In response to this, companies are working hard to develop new materials and methods of using these to increase the precision of a watch while keeping it at a reasonable price.

Negative Imapct Of Technology In Watch Industry

With every positive side in it there is a hidden negative side too. If technology has brought positive impact then with that it has also brought up the negative imapct too. So, let's discuss about the negative imapact of technology in the field of smart watches.

  • Data Protection Concerns

    • Devices like smartwatches and fitness trackers collects all of our personal details like health data, and here comes the doubt about the data protection and data security. Users need to be alert about the data being collected, stored and used and take appropriate measures to protecdt their privacy.

  • Bluetooth and WLAN radiations

    • Every digital devices generates the elctromagnetic radiations and electromagnetic fields. Smartwatches and fitness trackers work with WLAN and bluetooth raidiation. If the body is permanently exposed to this radiation it can have detrimental effect on our health over time.More than thousand scientific studies have confirmed it.

  • Shorter Battery Life

    • Shorter battery life may be a general issue of smartwatches. Digital watches use more power, necessitating more frequent battery changes or recharging. Since we need to charge the watch frequently it can lead to the more use of power.
